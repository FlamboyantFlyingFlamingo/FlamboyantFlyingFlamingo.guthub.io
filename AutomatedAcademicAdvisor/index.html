What is An Automated Academic Adivsor?

This tool was created to provide a more convenient experience for students in the process of scheduling their degree programme. A GPA calculator, Course Catalog and a strategic scheduling program are the three main functionalities provided by this instrusment.

What To Expect

As mentioned above, the three advantageous functions of this software will be the Course Catalog, The Scheduling Program and The GPA Calculator

Course Catalog

Current and prospective undergraduate students can view a list of the required courses for the various programmes offered by the university.

Scheduling Program

Students will possess the ability to closely monitor the required courses they must complete .

GPA Calculator

The GPA Calculator helps students monitor their academic performance & scholarship eligibility.
